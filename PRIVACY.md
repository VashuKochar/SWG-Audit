# Privacy Policy

SWG Audit ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy when you use our website and services (the "Service").

## Information we collect

### Information you provide

- **Business email:** When you verify to access simulations, we collect the business email you enter. It is used only for access control and abuse prevention. We do not send verification emails in the MVP.
- **Captcha:** We use Google reCAPTCHA. Googleâ€™s privacy policy applies to reCAPTCHA data: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Session cookie:** We set a signed cookie to remember that you have verified. The cookie does not contain your email or other personal data.
- **Uploaded files:** If you use the data exfiltration simulation, we temporarily store uploaded files and delete them automatically after a short period (e.g. 10 minutes).

### Automatically collected information

We may collect information such as IP address and request logs in server logs. We do not use third-party analytics cookies in the MVP.

## How we use your information

- To provide and operate the Service.
- To control access to simulations (verification gate).
- To prevent abuse.

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information.

## Data retention

- Uploaded files: deleted automatically after a short period (e.g. 10 minutes).
- Session cookie: expires after a set period (e.g. 24 hours).
- We do not retain your business email after the session for the MVP.

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these, please open an issue in the repository or contact the maintainers.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the [repository](https://github.com/swgauditor/swgaudit).
